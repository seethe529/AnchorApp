# Privacy Policy for Anchor - PTSD Support

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Anchor ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device
- Mood logs and emotional check-ins
- Safety plan information
- Technique usage history
- Conversation history with AI support
- App preferences and settings

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers
- We do not track your location beyond what you explicitly provide
- We do not share your data with third parties
- We do not sell your information

## How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:
- To provide personalized mental health support
- To track your progress and mood patterns
- To remember your preferences and settings
- To improve your experience with the app

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI API (Optional)
If you use the AI Support feature, your messages are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this data: https://openai.com/privacy

### Expo Services
We use Expo for app development and updates. Expo may collect anonymous usage data. See Expo's privacy policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

## Data Security

- All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- Data is stored only on your device
- No cloud backups are created without your explicit consent
- You can delete all your data at any time from Settings

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all data stored by the app
- Delete all your data at any time
- Export your data (future feature)
- Opt out of any optional features

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Medical Disclaimer

Anchor is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of qualified health providers with questions regarding medical conditions. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, contact emergency services immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
